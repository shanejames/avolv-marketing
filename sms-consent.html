About Our SMS Messaging Program

Avolv.ai is an AI-powered business automation platform that enables small and medium businesses to communicate with their customers via SMS text messaging. This page describes our SMS messaging program, how consent is obtained, the messages users will receive, and how to opt out.

Messages sent through the Avolv.ai platform fall into the following categories:

  • Appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Service job notifications and status updates
  • Estimate and invoice delivery
  • Post-service follow-up communications
  • Customer satisfaction surveys
  • Marketing promotions (only to customers who have explicitly opted in)

How Consent Is Obtained

Customers provide consent to receive SMS messages through the methods described below. The Avolv.ai platform records and stores consent with a timestamp, the IP address of the request, the consent language version shown to the user, and the method of opt-in.

Web Form Opt-In (Primary Method)

When creating an account, booking an appointment, or requesting a service through a business's website or application powered by Avolv.ai, customers enter their phone number and actively check an unchecked consent box. The exact disclosure language displayed above the submit button is shown in the example form above. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Verbal Consent

Customers may provide verbal consent when scheduling appointments or requesting services over the phone. The Avolv.ai team member or business operator reads the consent disclosure to the customer and logs the customer's verbal agreement, the timestamp, and the staff member's name in the Avolv.ai platform.

Keyword Opt-In

Customers can text START, SUBSCRIBE, or YES to a business's Avolv.ai-provisioned phone number to opt in to receiving text messages.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the type of communication and the services being provided. Customers typically receive 1-10 messages per month depending on their service schedule and communication preferences. Marketing messages, where applicable, are limited to no more than 4 per month and are sent only to customers who have explicitly opted in to marketing communications.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Avolv.ai does not charge customers for SMS messages, but your wireless carrier may.

How to Opt Out

To stop receiving text messages at any time, simply reply:

STOP

to any message you receive from us.

After texting STOP, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. Additional opt-out keywords accepted: OPTOUT, CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, REVOKE, STOPALL. You may opt back in at any time by texting START.
