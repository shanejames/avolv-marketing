Honest answers

Questions actual pool guys ask us.

"I already use Skimmer / Pool Service Software. Why switch?"+
Skimmer is great for the chemical side of pool service. Avolv runs the front of your business: an AI estimator that reads customer videos and drafts quotes in seconds, a real CRM pipeline, marketing automation, voice-to-invoice from the truck, voice notes and reminders. Most customers either keep Skimmer for water chemistry alongside Avolv or switch entirely. Side-by-side, you replace 4 or 5 separate apps with one.
"How long does setup take?"+
About 5 business days from "yes" to live. We do it for you: phone number provisioning, A2P SMS registration, website setup, service catalog import, team accounts, and a 60-minute training call. You don't have to be technical.
"What if the AI sends a wrong estimate?"+
It doesn't send. It drafts. Every AI-generated quote sits in your pipeline waiting for you to tap "send" or edit first. Your service catalog defines the price ranges, so the draft can't go outside your guardrails. Once you trust it, you can flip on auto-send for specific service types.
"Will my customers know they're texting a bot?"+
They know your business answered fast and got them a quote. We don't impersonate humans, but we also don't announce "Hi, I'm a bot" every text. The AI uses your business voice, signs messages with your business name, and hands off to you the moment a question gets technical or out of scope.
"How much does Avolv cost?"+
It depends on your business. One truck or fifteen, pool service or cleaning, the platform is the same but how we package it for you is different. We don't publish numbers because we'd rather give you a real fit on a 15-minute call than a one-size-fits-all sticker price. What we will tell you upfront: there are no per-truck fees, no per-seat fees, no setup fees, and no contracts. You pay one monthly price, you get everything we build, and you can cancel any time.
"What does Avolv replace?"+
Most pool service owners we talk to are using 4 or 5 separate tools: a scheduling app, QuickBooks, a separate texting app, an AI receptionist or answering service, a website, and Zapier to glue it all together. That's typically $400-$700/month, plus the hours every week reconciling between them. Avolv replaces all of it in one app, with everything talking to everything else. Most owners find Avolv pays for itself within the first 30 days just from the tools they cancel.
"Is my customer data safe?"+
Yes. Multi-tenant database with row-level isolation, encrypted at rest and in transit, hosted on Render with daily backups. Every Avolv customer's data is walled off from every other customer's. We're working toward SOC 2 Type II for 2026.
"What if I'm not technical?"+
Most of our customers aren't. Our current operators run everything from their phones. We built Avolv specifically because the existing tools were either too simple (Jobber-lite) or too complex (ServiceTitan). If you can run a pool route, you can run Avolv.
